motion language into Acts II–III. The page ships and works with **zero** of them
(fallbacks are live); slot them in as they're produced.

**Continuity requirements for every clip**
- Exact same flat 2D-illustration style as `capptions.mp4`: coral cartoon brain
  character (thick navy outlines, teal tear-drops), paper-white background
  (#FBFCFD) with faint teal sparkles, coral/teal/navy/gold palette.
- 1920×1080 · 25 fps · no motion blur (it smears when scrubbed/paused).
- No baked-in text or logos other than the round coral capptions mark.
- Loops must be seamless (first frame == last frame). No audio.
